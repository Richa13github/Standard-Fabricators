About

Standard Fabricators was established in 1963 by Shamrao Mohite.Shamrao Mohite laid the roots of his industry in Kolhapur at Shivaji Udyam Nagar. He started his arduous journey moving to various places ,acquiring knowledge and established his own industrial unit at Kolhapur.He started manufacturing rolling shutters and various fabrications.His scrupulous nature helped him manufacture various equipments and machines like bottom guide machine and rolling shutter plate manufacturing machine.After his demise his legacy is being carried forward by his daughters who have etched their names as the successfull entrepreneurs in town.
